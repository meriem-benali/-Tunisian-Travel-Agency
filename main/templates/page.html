Embark on a Magical Journey to Tunisia: Where Dreams Come Alive!

Welcome to Tunisia, your gateway to an unforgettable journey filled with history, adventure, and relaxation.

Explore Ancient Wonders: Immerse yourself in the ancient ruins of Carthage, walk through the legendary amphitheater of El Djem, and wander through the labyrinthine streets of ancient Medina.

Bask in Natural Beauty: Relax on sun-drenched beaches along the Mediterranean coast, or venture into the breathtaking landscapes of the Sahara Desert, where golden sands meet endless horizons.

Experience Vibrant Culture: Lose yourself in bustling souks filled with vibrant colors and exotic scents, indulge in mouthwatering cuisine bursting with Mediterranean flavors, and immerse yourself in the warmth and hospitality of Tunisia's people

Prepare to be spellbound by the wonders of Tunisia. Your extraordinary adventure awaits.

Amphitheatre al jam

Amphitheatre al jam

The El Jem amphitheater is an exceptional testimony to Roman architecture, particularly that of monuments built for spectacle purposes, in Africa. Located in a plain in the center of Tunisia, this amphitheater, built entirely of cut stone, is neither dug nor backed by a hill.

Pirate tour

If you find yourself in Djerba and want to experience its magical side, a visit to Flamingo Island via a vintage pirate ship is mandatory. During this trip, you can enjoy a show presented by professional pirate artists. Passengers would be captivated by crystal clear blue sea, again enjoying special local and international music. Sousse Archaeological Museum

Sousse Archaeological Museum

The Sousse Archaeological Museum invites you to travel back in time. Its collection of Roman mosaics makes it one of the most important museums in the Mediterranean basin. Its renovation has beautified this space and further highlighted the objects on display. We also hope to see the renovation of other historical and cultural spaces in order to preserve and highlight our heritage even more.
